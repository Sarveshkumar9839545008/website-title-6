Republic Day Speech in English

On the occasion of the 75th Republic Day, I am honoured to address this esteemed gathering. Today, as we celebrate Republic Day, let us renew our commitment to our nation – to uphold the values enshrined in our Constitution. Republic Day 2024 Long Speech

Fellow Citizens, It is a day of immense pride and joy as we commemorate the historic moment when India’s Constitution came into effect on January 26, 1950, marking the country’s transition to a sovereign, democratic, and republic state. This day holds profound significance in the hearts of every Indian, as it symbolises the culmination of our hard-fought struggle for independence and the dawning of a new era of hope, equality, and justice.

Republic Day 2024 Short Speech

Ladies and gentlemen, esteemed guests, and fellow citizens. Today, as we gather to celebrate the 75th Republic Day of India, we are reminded of the immense pride and gratitude we should feel for our nation. This day marks the transition of India from a dominion to a sovereign, democratic, and republic state, and it is a testament to the hard work, dedication, and sacrifices of our freedom fighters and visionaries.
